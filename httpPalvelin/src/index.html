Work

I have been working as a freelance AI coding trainer for Scale AI under their platform Outlier since April 2025. I have worked in roles: Contributor, Reviewer, Senior reviewer and Briefly a QC Auditor.

At Outlier my responsibilities included:

  • Analyzing and rating code and fixing possible errors (syntax, logic, etc.)
  • Producing SOTA (state of the art) responses to prompts, including analyzing provided code's logic and developing scripts/smaller programs
  • Later as a reviewer, responsible for reviewing other contributors' ratings and fixing them
  • As a QC (Quality Control) Auditor our team was responsible for every programming projects quality, which we kept up by auditing batches of tasks before they were sent to the customer. The work was similar to a reviewer, the main differences being that we audited all programming projects and didn't fix any found mistakes.
  • I mainly worked with Java, JavaScript, and very rarely Python

I have also worked at the University of Jyväskylä as a teaching assistant in the course Programming 1 and 2, where my job was to teach and help new students with learning C# and Java, general programming concepts (also object-oriented programming in programming 2), rate their course projects and supervise exams. In addition, I was part of the development of the university's C# game-making library Jypeli.
