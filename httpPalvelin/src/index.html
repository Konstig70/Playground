Works

I have been working as a freelance AI coding trainer for Scale AI under their platform Outlier since April 2025.

At Outlier my responsibilities included:

  • Analyzing and rating code and fixing possible errors (syntax, logic, etc.)
  • Producing SOTA (state of the art) responses to prompts, including analyzing provided code's logic and developing scripts/smaller programs
  • Later as a reviewer, responsible for reviewing other contributors' ratings and fixing them
  • Mainly worked with Java, JavaScript, and very rarely Python

I have also worked at the University of Jyväskylä as a teaching assistant in the course Programming 1, where my job was to teach and help new students with learning C# and general programming concepts and also rate their final projects. In addition, I was part of the development of the university's C# game-making library Jypeli.
